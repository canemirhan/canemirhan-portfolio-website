About Me

I was born in January 2003 in Küçükçekmece, Istanbul. Half of life has been spent in Istanbul and the other half in Bursa. Primary school and the first half of middle school were completed in Istanbul, while the second half of middle school and high school were finished in Bursa. I am currently back in Istanbul for my university studies. I am continuing my studies in the Computer Engineering department at Marmara University.

With a focus on advancing as an AI/ML Engineer, the goal is to become an exemplary engineer who touches people's lives. Progress is driven by discipline and determination, with a constant effort to gain new perspectives, create value, and contribute meaningfully to the field.
